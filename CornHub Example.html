CORNHUB INDUSTRY

Corn is the world's most important grain, based on production volume. In 2018/2019, a production level of around 1.09 billion metric tons of corn was achieved. In 2019/20 the United States was by far the biggest corn producer worldwide, with a production volume amounting to over 345 million metric tons.

Popcorn

Popcorn is a variety of corn kernel which expands and puffs up when heated; the same names are also used to refer to the foodstuff produced by the expansion. Sweet Corn

Sweet corn is a variety of maize grown for human consumption with a high sugar content. Sweet corn is the result of a naturally occurring recessive mutation in the genes which control conversion of sugar to starch inside the endosperm of the corn kernel.

Chalado

A cholado is a creamy marriage of a snow cone and a fruit cocktail. This combination of fruit syrup, sweetened condensed milk, and tropical fruits over crushed ice is a refreshing, favorite way to cool off on a warm day in Colombia.
